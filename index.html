About

Our Story

I came from an exclusive church that, like many, taught that salvation was found only in that organization. While I was raised by loving parents and most members were good people, I witnessed both the benefits and the harm of this system.

I'm not a musician by training, but God has inspired these songs in an unlikely way. As someone who grew up where "worldly music" was forbidden, it's remarkable that I've been able to create these songs at all.

These songs were created to help people from exclusive church backgrounds discover that salvation is through Jesus Christ alone—not through any organization or group of people.

Why Anonymous?

This ministry is about Jesus Christ and His sacrifice for us. It's not about any individual. We want the focus to remain on Him.

The Music
